How the Tunes page works

The page has two panels: Now Playing (player) and Library (tracks). On mobile, the Now Playing panel shows first and the Library stacks below it.

Tips

  • Pick a track under Quick tracks or choose a hymn to load a song.
  • Play/Pause controls the built-in audio player; Restart jumps back to the beginning.
  • Spotify player swaps into the Now Playing panel and replaces the built-in controls until you close it.
  • Use the collapse icon in each panel header to hide/show that panel’s contents.
